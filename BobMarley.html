Bob Marley

When someone says Reggae music usually people think Bob Marley, the King of Reggae. Bob Marley was the highlight of Reggae music in the mid 20th century. He made some of the calmest Reggae songs when it came to calming down others. Songs like: Stir it up, Mellow Mood, and Positive Vibrations. Songs like these would have calmed you down in times of unwanted stress. No list of reggae artists would be complete without Bob Marley in the top spot.
